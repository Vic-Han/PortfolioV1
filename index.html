2022 Canmore Nordic Centre Half Marathon
I'm obsessed with pushing myself as far as I can. I firmly believe that our biggest limits are the ones we put on ourselves. Ultra running is my way of stepping as far outside of my comfort zone as possible. It's how I show myself that I truly am someone who won't give up, no matter what. It's my philosophy that being confident can change everything we do, and that we earn this confidence by overcoming our self doubts. While all endurance sports are challenging and lead to moments of self-doubt, I believe running is the most brutal.
I was inspired by a coworker to attempt a 100km bike ride. At the time, it seemed impossible as my longest ride had only been 40km! However, as I continued to train, I quickly realized how foolish my doubts were. While 5-hour training sessions were too stressful on my body when running, cycling allowed me to do so every weekend. I often set ambitious time goals, and I was forced to find another level of effort during these rides. I learned the power of being able to talk myself through a difficult situation, whether on the road or in real life.
